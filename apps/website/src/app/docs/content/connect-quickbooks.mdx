Connect Quadra to QuickBooks Online to export your categorized transactions to your accounting software.

## How it works

Once connected:

1. Transactions arrive from your bank and receipts are matched automatically
2. Ready transactions appear in the **Review** tab (the export queue)
3. Export to QuickBooks — categories map to your chart of accounts
4. Exported transactions are marked as **Exported** in Quadra

[Learn about the full export flow →](/docs/export-transactions-csv)

## Connect QuickBooks

1. Go to [Apps](https://app-quadra.vercel.app/apps)
2. Find **QuickBooks**
3. Click **Connect**
4. Sign in to your QuickBooks account
5. Select your QuickBooks company
6. Authorize Quadra

## Category mapping

Quadra categories map to QuickBooks accounts:

- Match each Quadra category to the appropriate QuickBooks account
- Mapping is configured once during setup
- You can update mappings in settings

Work with your accountant to ensure the mappings match your chart of accounts.

## What gets exported

- Transaction date
- Amount
- Description/merchant name
- Category (mapped to QuickBooks account)
- Attached receipts (as files)

## Exporting transactions

1. Go to the **Review** tab in [Transactions](https://app-quadra.vercel.app/transactions)
2. Select the transactions you want to export (or export all)
3. Click **Export** and choose **QuickBooks**
4. Transactions and receipts are sent to QuickBooks with your category mappings applied
5. Exported transactions are marked as **Exported** in Quadra

## Disconnect

To remove the connection:

1. Go to [Apps](https://app-quadra.vercel.app/apps)
2. Click **Disconnect** on QuickBooks

Previously exported data remains in QuickBooks.

## Tips

- Work with your accountant to set up correct category mappings
- Review the first few exported transactions in QuickBooks to verify accuracy
- Keep receipts attached in Quadra—they export with transactions
- Export regularly (weekly or monthly) to keep QuickBooks current
