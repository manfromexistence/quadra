Project Title

Develop a QUADRA (Electronic Document Management System) – Web Application

Project Description

We are looking for an experienced Full Stack Developer or Development Team to build a modern Electronic Document Management System (EDMS) specifically designed for civil construction projects.

The system will allow Clients, PMC (Project Management Consultants), Vendors, and Subcontractors to upload, review, approve, and track project documents efficiently.

The objective is to create a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that is scalable, secure, and user-friendly, with the ability to expand into a full enterprise system.
